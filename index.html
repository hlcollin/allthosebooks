dear fellow grc student,

YOU KNOW ALL THOSE BOOKS IN THE RESOURCE ROOM
THAT NO ONE EVER LOOKS AT?

I mean the really, really old ones. The ones where the binding is falling off and the endpaper probably smells like tobacco. Yeah, well those books actually have really, really, cool pictures inside. And I believe that your college experience will be significantly diminished if you do not look at them.

BUT LET'S BE REAL

no one in their right mind is ever going to actually get up, walk over, pick up, and open a book with their hands. So out of the goodness of my heart, I have done all the hard work for you. I chose seven random old books, found the best images in each of them, and then put them all on this website for you to enjoy. No getting up required, all you have to do is...

click.

yours truly,

heather collins
